Presentational element

The <bold> tag is used to draw attention to text without indicating that it's more important.

While <strong> tag is for content that is of greater importance.

The <italic> tag display text in italic.

While <emphasis> tag represents stress emphasis of contents.

Use of <mark> element. Presentation (or Style) refers to anything related to how the content and structure is presented. Examples: size, color, margins, borders, layout, location, etc.
